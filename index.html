CUNY Bronx Community College is a public institution in Bronx, New York. Its campus is located in a city with a total enrollment of 8,370. The school utilizes a semester-based academic year. The student-faculty ratio is 18-to-1. The highest degree offered at CUNY Bronx Community College is an associate degree. The school has an open admissions policy.
